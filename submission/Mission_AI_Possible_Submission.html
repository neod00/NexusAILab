💡 Idea, Background & History

💭 The Idea

In 2025, LRQA Korea's sales team identified a critical bottleneck: the manual ISO certification application process was slow, error-prone, and language-limited, creating friction in customer acquisition. What if we could apply AI to eliminate these barriers and create a seamless, intelligent experience?

📖 Background

LRQA is a global leader in certification, inspection, and training services, serving 61,000+ clients across 160+ countries. Despite our expertise, our application process relied on PDFs, emails, and manual data entry – technology from the 2000s serving 2025 clients.

🔬 Research Phase

We analyzed 500+ application submissions and discovered: 30-45 min average completion time, 15-20% error rate requiring rework, 3-5 days for quotation delivery, and zero after-hours support. International clients struggled with Korean/English-only forms.

🛠️ Development

Starting May 2025, our Nexus AI Lab team built a proof-of-concept using OpenAI GPT-4, Google Gemini, Google Sheets API, and serverless functions. Within 5 months, we launched a working platform with AI chatbot, auto-save, document generation, and 10-language support.

🧪 Testing & Validation

Internal testing phase in Sep 2025 processed 94 test applications with 89% completion rate. Internal staff feedback: "Like having a consultant available 24/7" and "Finally, a form that speaks my language." Average time: 12 minutes (down from 40).

🎯 Vision

This is just the beginning. Our roadmap includes 40+ ISO standards, industry-specific templates, mobile apps, blockchain certificates, and AI-powered audit report/Scheduling analysis. We're not just digitizing forms – we're reimagining certification services for the AI era.

"We didn't set out to build software. We set out to solve a real problem our clients faced every day. AI was the tool; better customer experience was the goal."
— LRQA Korea Nexus AI Lab Team

⚖️ Risks & Mitigation Strategies

🔒 Data Security & Privacy

Risk: Sensitive client data exposure

Mitigation: SSL/HTTPS encryption, GDPR compliance, Google OAuth 2.0, regular security audits

🤖 AI Accuracy & Reliability

Risk: Chatbot providing incorrect information

Mitigation: Human-in-the-loop review, continuous training on ISO documentation, fallback to human agents

🔗 System Integration Complexity

Risk: Google Sheets API failures or sync errors

Mitigation: Robust error handling, automatic retry logic, local storage fallback, monitoring alerts

👥 User Adoption & Training

Risk: Staff resistance to new technology

Mitigation: Comprehensive training programs, intuitive UX design, gradual rollout, ongoing support
