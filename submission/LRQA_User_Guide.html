üìù Application Form Guide

The application form is divided into multiple sections. Each section collects specific information required for ISO certification processing.

Section 1: Company Information

Required Fields:

  • Company Name: Official registered business name
  • Business Registration Number: Government-issued registration ID
  • Industry Type: Select from dropdown list
  • Year Established: Company founding year
  • Number of Employees: Current total headcount
üí° Tip

Have your business registration document ready for reference when filling out this section.

Section 2: Contact Information

Required Fields:

  • Primary Contact Name: Person responsible for certification
  • Job Title: Position within the company
  • Email Address: Valid business email (will be used for all communications)
  • Phone Number: Direct contact number with country code
  • Office Address: Full business address including postal code
  • Website: Company website URL (optional but recommended)

Section 3: Certification Requirements

Select the ISO standard(s) you're applying for. You can select multiple standards:

  • ISO 9001: Quality Management System
  • ISO 14001: Environmental Management System
  • ISO 45001: Occupational Health & Safety Management System

For each selected standard, provide:

  • Scope of Certification: Products/services to be covered
  • Number of Sites: Locations to be certified
  • Current Status: New application or re-certification

Section 4: Employee Information

Provide breakdown of employees by category:

  • Management/Executive
  • Office/Administrative
  • Production/Manufacturing
  • Technical/Engineering
  • Part-time/Contract workers
‚ÑπÔ∏è Why We Need This

Employee numbers help determine audit duration and certification costs. Include all employees across all sites.

Section 5: Existing Certifications (if any)

If your company already holds ISO certifications:

  • Certification body name
  • Certificate number
  • Issue and expiry dates
  • Scope of existing certification

Section 6: Consultant Information (Optional)

If you're working with an ISO consultant:

  • Consultant/consulting firm name
  • Consultant contact details
  • Services provided by consultant

Section 7: Data Consent

Review and accept:

  • Data collection and retention policy
  • Privacy and confidentiality terms
  • Communication preferences
